About Us

Welcome to Magic-95 Foundation

We are committed to transforming lives through sustainable and impactful initiatives, focusing on rural development, climate change awareness, organic farming, and various community-driven projects. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities, foster self-reliance, and build a brighter future for all.

Our Services

Explore Our Services

Rural Development

We promote sustainable development in rural areas, helping communities grow and thrive.
Climate Change

Addressing the impact of climate change with sustainable practices and awareness programs.
Organic Farming

Promoting eco-friendly farming methods for healthier produce and a cleaner environment.
Self Help Groups (SHA)

Empowering individuals through self-help groups to foster collaboration and support.
Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO)

Helping farmers enhance their productivity and market access through organized efforts.
Microfinancing

Providing financial services to small businesses and entrepreneurs to drive growth.
Ayush Projects

Promoting holistic health practices through Ayush systems including Ayurveda and Yoga.
De-addiction Center

Offering support and rehabilitation for individuals battling addiction issues.
Family Counseling

Providing counseling services to support families in overcoming challenges.
Disabled Projects

Supporting individuals with disabilities through various development programs.
Mentally Literate Projects

Fostering mental health awareness and literacy through dedicated projects.
Tribal Projects

Enhancing the lives of tribal communities through focused development initiatives.

Why People Trust Us!

Key Reasons Why People Trust Us in Community Development!

We are dedicated to fostering sustainable growth and improving the lives of individuals and communities. Our commitment to transparency, empowerment, and community involvement has earned us the trust of many.

Sustainable Development

Our focus on organic farming and sustainable practices helps preserve the environment while empowering local communities.

Community Empowerment

We actively involve local communities in projects like rural development, Ayush projects, and family counseling to ensure their needs are met.

Financial Inclusion

Through microfinancing and support for Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), we enhance economic opportunities for marginalized groups.
